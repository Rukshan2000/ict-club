About Us - Cybernetics ICT Day

Cybernetics ICT Day celebrates the dynamic intersection of technology and human interaction. Through hands-on workshops and engaging sessions, we explore Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), cybernetics, AI, web design, and programming. Our goal is to equip students with the skills and knowledge to understand and shape the future of technology in the digital age.

Join us for an exciting experience where technology and innovation meet! Our event brings together students, professionals, and enthusiasts to engage in discussions and activities that push the boundaries of technology.

Through interactive workshops, we provide a platform for students to enhance their skills in web development, AI, programming, and more. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, Cybernetics ICT Day is the perfect opportunity to learn, grow, and network with like-minded individuals.
